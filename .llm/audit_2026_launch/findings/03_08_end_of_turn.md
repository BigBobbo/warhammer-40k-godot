## Top 3 launch-blocker end-of-turn gaps

1. **`da_jump_used_this_turn` never resets — Weirdboys are crippled after one Da Jump (#8 above).**
   Wahapedia text: "Once per turn, at the end of your Movement phase…". Once the flag flips to `true`, `MovementPhase.gd:2092` rejects every subsequent Da Jump for the rest of the game. Live-reproduced through two turn boundaries. Affects every Ork roster shipping a Weirdboy (currently `orks.json`, `Orks_2000.json`, `Orks_2000_upload.json`, `Orks_Upload_Mar7.json`, `ORK_test.json`). Fix: add `"da_jump_used_this_turn"` to `flags_to_reset` in BOTH `phases/ScoringPhase.gd:332-341` AND `autoloads/GameManager.gd:1107-1112`, OR migrate to the `Scatter!` pattern (manager-side dict cleared at next-Movement-phase entry).

2. **Single-player vs multiplayer end-of-turn flag-reset divergence (#10) — and Round-3 reserves destruction missing in MP (#25).**
   Two parallel implementations of "end-of-turn cleanup" exist (`ScoringPhase._handle_end_turn` for single-player; `GameManager.process_end_scoring` for multiplayer). The MP path resets 11 of the 18 flags the SP path resets and skips the Round-3 reserves-destroy block entirely. Concrete consequences in MP: leaked `has_fought` could mis-order a unit's next Fight phase, `burned_objective` / `performed_ritual` / `performed_terraform` could double-score, and reserves units that should be destroyed at end of Round 3 stay alive. Fix: make `GameManager.process_end_scoring` delegate to `ScoringPhase._handle_end_turn` (single source of truth) instead of forking the cleanup logic.

3. **Only one of ten "at the end of your opponent's turn" datasheet abilities is wired (#12).**
   `Acrobatic Escape` (Callidus) has the full eligibility-scan + signal + dialog path. `From Golden Light` (Custodes — IN AN ACTIVE ROSTER), `Airborne Agility`, `Death From Below`, `Skyleap`, `Hunters from the Warp`, `Ambushing Hunters`, `Guerrilla Tactics`, `Cloudstrider`, `Webway Shunt Generator`, `Teleportation Matrix` — none fire. They are nine identical patterns ("if not within ER, remove and place into Strategic Reserves") that could share a single generic handler in `ScoringPhase._handle_end_turn`. `From Golden Light` is the priority because it is on the `Adeptus_Custodes_1995_Mar_7.json` user-uploaded roster.

## Top 3 invisible features

1. **`From Golden Light`** (and its 8 sibling abilities — see #12). Datasheet text exists, ability is referenced in active Custodes roster JSON, no engine wiring. Player has no UI affordance to invoke.

2. **`Infiltrator Comms Array`** (#20). Declared `implemented: true, once_per_turn: true` in `UnitAbilityManager.gd:1050-1060` with a `regain_cp_on_5plus` effect — but no caller anywhere in the codebase. The `implemented: true` claim is a pin-test-style declaration unsupported by behavior.

3. **`Desiccation Conduit (Aura)`** (#13). Death Guard datasheet aura that deals D3 mortal wounds to enemies in Contagion Range "at the end of the turn". Catalog rows present, zero engine references. Currently no Death Guard rosters fielded so impact is breadth-coverage rather than launch-blocking, but the rule is real and should be wired before Death Guard is added.
