Method. Issues are worked in dependency order from ISSUES.md; every change lands with its own regression tests (the gate grew from 562 → 943 checks), windowed-scenario validation over the in-game MCP bridge where player-facing, and an individual commit. The 63-entry golden corpus pins both attack-resolution paths byte-for-byte, and the edition switch (GameConstants.edition, default 10) lets every 11th-edition behavior land provably without touching the playable 10e game. The rulebook's own worked examples are the acceptance fixtures — seven of them now reproduce exactly in tests.
